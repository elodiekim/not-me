# NotMe Product Specification

Version: 1.0

---

# Product Overview

## Product Name

NotMe

## Tagline

**Uber for Weird Problems**

---

## Introduction

NotMe is a mobile-first platform that connects people facing weird, awkward, and urgent household problems with nearby local heroes.

Some problems are too small for professionals.

Some problems are too awkward to ask friends.

Some problems simply need someone brave enough to help.

NotMe exists for those moments.

---

# Vision

Become the first app people open when they encounter a weird household problem.

---

# Mission

Make asking for small, awkward, or unusual help as easy as ordering a ride.

---

# Why NotMe Exists

There are countless apps for food delivery, transportation, cleaning, and moving.

But there is no simple platform for situations like:

- A cockroach suddenly appears.
- A giant spider is on the ceiling.
- A bee flies into the room.
- A heavy box is impossible to lift alone.
- A light bulb is too high to replace.
- Something strange happens and you simply need help.

NotMe exists to solve these weird everyday moments.

---

# Core Concept

NotMe is **not** a general marketplace.

NotMe is **not** a professional service platform.

NotMe specializes in weird household problems.

The experience should always feel:

- Fast
- Friendly
- Stress-free
- Human

- Busy office workers

- Anyone uncomfortable dealing with insects

Hero Users

- Local residents
- Students

# User Roles

## User

The person requesting help.

Responsibilities

- Create requests
- Track request status
- Communicate if necessary
- Leave reviews

---

## Hero

The person accepting requests.

Responsibilities

- Accept nearby missions
- Complete the task
- Build trust
- Receive rewards

---

# MVP

The MVP focuses on validating one hypothesis.

**People are willing to use an app to request immediate cockroach removal from nearby helpers.**

Everything else is secondary.

# Success Metrics

The product is successful if:

- Users understand the app within 3 seconds.
- Request creation takes less than 10 seconds.
- Mission acceptance feels immediate.
- The experience feels trustworthy and simple.

# Product Principles

Every screen should have one primary action.

Reduce unnecessary decisions.

Reduce typing.

Reduce waiting.

Reduce anxiety.

Keep interactions simple.

---

# Future Vision

Over time, NotMe becomes the platform people trust whenever something weird happens at home.

The platform expands carefully while keeping its identity.

Not every household problem needs a professional.

Sometimes you just need someone nearby.

---

# Non Goals

NotMe is NOT:

- Food Delivery
- Cleaning Service
- Professional Pest Control
- Moving Company
- General Marketplace
- TaskRabbit Clone

The product should remain focused on weird household problems.

---

# Definition of MVP Success

The MVP succeeds when users can:

- Create a request
- Receive a hero
- Complete the mission
- Leave a review

If those four experiences feel smooth, the MVP is successful.
